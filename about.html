Our Story

Ruby was founded in 2023 with a simple mission: to create a platform where writers and readers can connect through meaningful content. We believe in the power of storytelling and the impact it can have on individuals and communities.

Our platform is designed to be clean, minimalist, and focused on what matters most - the content. We've eliminated distractions to provide an immersive reading experience that allows ideas to shine.

Our Values

  • Quality: We prioritize well-crafted content over quantity
  • Community: Building connections between writers and readers
  • Innovation: Continuously improving the reading experience
  • Accessibility: Making content available to everyone
